I first started sketching wireframes in elementary school to help my grandmother learn new technologies because the mental models were quite a struggle for her grasp. Years later, her experiences have influenced me to practice UX design.

I create measurable impact for organizations and lasting customer relationships though a human-centered process. I support my design decisions with data-driven insights about people's behaviors to simplify their lived experiences.

What I value as a designer ...


Put people before technology


Solve for the right problem


Promote collaboration & psychological safety


Learn quickly & learn often
